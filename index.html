For Sam

Hey you. I know it’s been a few days, but I’ve been working on something. This? This is for you. Take your time. Read it slow. It’s all yours.

The way your eyes light up when you're explaining something you love — it gets me every time.

What I wish you knew…

Even when you think you’re being quiet or distant, I still feel so connected to you. You don’t have to try — just being you is enough.

What I’d tell you if you were here right now…

Come closer. I’ve missed the sound of your voice, the way your hand fits in mine. Stay a while, okay?

You’re my favorite quiet moment and loudest desire all at once.
